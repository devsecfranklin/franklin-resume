\noindent Franklin Diaz is a results-driven Principal Engineer, security researcher, and educator with over 20 years of experience spanning telecommunications, software development, cloud security, and DevSecOps. Alongside his corporate consulting, Franklin is a highly active member of the open-source security community and serves as an Adjunct Instructor at the University of Colorado Denver Business School, bridging the gap between industry practice and academic theory.
