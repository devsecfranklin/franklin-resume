Franklin Diaz is a Principal Engineer at Palo Alto Networks. He specializes in public cloud infrastructure, automation, and DevSecOps, and is a member of the Professional Services team. He works to create infrastructure as code and pipelines for security and build data, including continuous integration, development, and deployment.

Diaz is a 20+ year technology professional with experience in Telecommunications, Networks, Software, and Information Security. He holds an MS in Computer Information Systems from Northwestern University and an MS in Network Engineering/Network Security from DePaul University in Chicago, as well as a BS in Computer Science.

His past roles include Staff Software Engineer at Motorola, Research & Development Engineer at Nokia Siemens Networks, and Security Data Engineer n the Detection Engineering & Analytics team at Salesforce.
