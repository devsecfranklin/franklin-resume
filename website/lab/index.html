VNC Connections

  • enable VNC in raspi-config
  • from the raspi desktop, change from unix auth to VNC auth
  • ssh -L 5901:127.0.0.1:5901 -C -N -l pi x.x.x.x
  • Once the tunnel is running, use a VNC client to connect to localhost:5901
